Abstraction Ladder

Every idea exists on a ladder from concrete → abstract. Moving up means generalising to a bigger point; moving down means grounding it in a real example. The best communicators move fluidly between both.

↑ Abstract
Values, principles
Big idea — conclusion or belief
"Social media is changing what we think success looks like."
· Middle
Pattern, observation
Observation — a trend or category
"People increasingly measure their own progress by comparing it to curated highlights online."
↓ Concrete
Fact, example
Specific fact — something verifiable
"A friend of mine got promoted last year and said it felt empty because it looked better on Instagram than it felt in real life."
Move up ↑ when? You are getting lost in detail — generalise to find common ground, bring everyone về cùng big picture.
Xuống thang ↓ khi nào? Ý kiến còn mơ hồ → đưa ví dụ cụ thể để thuyết phục và tránh bị hỏi ngược.
PREP: Point (Abstract) → Reason (Middle) → Example (Concrete) → Point (Abstract lại).
SITA: Situation/Issue (Concrete) → Take (Middle/Abstract) → Ask (align toàn team).
2 cấu trúc trả lời phổ biến

Structure A · P-R-E-P

Dùng cho câu hỏi ý kiến, thảo luận, phản biện lịch sự trong meeting.
P
Point — Quan điểm
"I think the best approach here is..."
R
Reason — Lý do
"Because it helps us reduce..."
E
Example — Ví dụ
"For instance, last month our team..."
P
Point — Tóm lại
"So that's why I'd recommend..."

Structure B · S-I-T-A

Dùng khi update tiến độ, báo cáo vấn đề, đề xuất giải pháp.
S
Situation — Bối cảnh
"Currently, we're at stage 2 of..."
I
Issue — Vấn đề
"The challenge we're facing is..."
T
Take — Đề xuất
"What I'd suggest is we..."
A
Ask — Xác nhận
"Does that work for everyone?"
💡 Làm giàu câu trả lời với 5W1H
Lồng các câu hỏi What / Why / Where / When / How / Who vào từng bước của PREP hoặc SITA để câu trả lời đầy đủ và thuyết phục hơn:
PREP · Point = What do I think? → Reason = Why? → Example = When/How did this happen? → Point = What is my conclusion?
SITA · Situation = What/Where/When? → Issue = What went wrong & Why? → Take = How to fix? → Ask = Who agrees?
